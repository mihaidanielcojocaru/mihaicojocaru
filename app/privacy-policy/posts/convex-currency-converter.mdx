# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 16, 2026

Convex - Currency Converter ("we," "our," or "us") provides a currency conversion app. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

## Information We Collect

- **Advertising Data:** The app displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use device information (such as advertising identifiers), IP address, and usage data to serve and measure ads.
- **Network Access:** The app uses an internet connection to fetch live exchange rates. We do not collect personal information for this purpose.

## What We Do Not Collect

- We do not require account creation.
- We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information.
- We do not store personal data locally in the app.

## Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob for advertising. Their data practices are governed by Google's policies.

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- AdMob: https://support.google.com/admob

## How We Use Information

Advertising data is used to deliver and measure ads. Exchange rate data is used only to provide conversion results.

## Children's Privacy

Convex - Currency Converter is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Choices

You can reset or limit the advertising identifier in your device settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted with a new effective date.
