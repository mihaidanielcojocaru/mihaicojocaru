Rider Dashboard turns your iPhone into a professional automotive dashboard with precise GPS-based measurements. Whether you are driving, riding, or testing performance, it delivers reliable, real-time data with up to 1-meter accuracy.

## Accurate Performance Metrics

### Precision Speedometer
Track real-time speed with automotive-grade GPS accuracy.

### Acceleration Times
Measure 0-60 km/h (0-37 mph) and 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph) runs with consistent, repeatable timing.

### Distance Benchmarks
Monitor 10 m, 100 m, and 201 m (1/8 mile) performance for quick comparative testing.

### Top Speed Tracking
Record your peak speed and review it at a glance.

## Built for Drivers and Riders

### Distraction-Minimizing Design
A clean, high-contrast interface stays legible in any light and keeps focus on the road.

### Always-On Display
Keep the screen active while tracking to avoid interruptions mid-run.

### Background Recording
Capture performance data even when the app is not in the foreground.

### Live GPS Accuracy
See real-time accuracy in meters so you know the quality of every measurement.

## Professional Dashboard Experience

### Analog + Digital Readouts
An analog-style dial pairs with large digital numbers for instant readability.

### Trip and Odometer Tracking
Track trip distance in imperial or metric units, plus a lifetime odometer total.

## Customizable Settings

### Unit Selection
Switch between km/h and mph with a single tap.

### Accuracy Controls
Adjust GPS accuracy thresholds based on your testing needs.

### Trip Management
Save and reset trip data independently for clean comparisons.

Rider Dashboard is ideal for automotive enthusiasts, riders, and track-day drivers who want more precise data than a built-in speedometer.
